General Surgery

Our team of surgical professionals at Cambridge Hospital provides highly personalised, comprehensive diagnostic services and surgical services.

Each patient is admitted under the care of a consultant, who directs your course of treatment with the support of a highly trained team of residents and nurses.

At Cambridge Hospital our entire team is committed to working together to provide you with expert, compassionate care before, during and after your surgery.

Our general surgery unit is on call 24 hours a day. Our surgeons have a broad range of specialties.We also offer a Surgical Outpatient Clinic.
