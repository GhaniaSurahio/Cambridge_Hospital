Who We Are?

The Cambridge Hospital campus is a community teaching hospital located in Cambridge,Behind GPO road near JS bank Street Larkana. The hospital is located at 1493 Cambridge Street, between Inman Square and Harvard Square. It is one of three hospital campuses part of Cambridge Health Alliance.
John Addenbrooke (1680-1719) was the founder of Addenbrooke's Hospital in Cambridge

Opened the first Cambridge Hospital in 1867 and was resident matron for three years. "Through her efforts the Cambridge Hospital was incorporated in 1871 and present building erected 1886."
